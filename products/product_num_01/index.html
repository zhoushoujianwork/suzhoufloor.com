    Vipp13

    Pedal bin 4 L / 1 gal

    The Vipp13 pedal bin the smallest of the Vipp bins and is a trash can ideal for the bathroom. This pedal bin is equipped with a removable inner bin, a lid that ensures air-tight closure, and a damper mechanism that provides smooth closing of the lid. A rubber ring at the base protects floors.     Step on it

    The pedal bin was not designed in the strict traditional sense, but got its visual expression in response to a series of functional criteria; one being a pedal for a hands-free operation.

    Ideal for small bathrooms

    Being the smallest of our bins, the Vipp13 pedal bin requires minimal space and thrives well in smaller bathrooms.
